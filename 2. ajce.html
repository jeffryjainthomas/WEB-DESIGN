Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

About Us

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE), located in Koovappally, Kottayam, is one of Kerala's premier engineering institutions. Established in 2001 and managed by the Catholic Diocese of Kanjirappally, the college is known for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and strong industry connections. Accredited by NBA and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), AJCE offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in various engineering disciplines. The campus boasts modern labs, a vibrant research culture, and a strong focus on innovation and entrepreneurship. With a high placement record and emphasis on holistic development, Amal Jyothi is a preferred choice for aspiring engineers in Kerala.
