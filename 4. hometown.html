Welcome to Kottayam

Kottayam is a beautiful town in Kerala, India.

Kottayam is a picturesque district in the Indian state of Kerala, known for its lush green landscapes, rubber plantations, and serene backwaters. Often called the "Land of Letters, Lakes, and Latex," it is a hub of education and literature, with many prominent publishing houses based here. The town is surrounded by hills like the Vagamon and Ilaveezhapoonchira, offering breathtaking views and trekking opportunities. The famous Vembanad Lake, the largest in Kerala, adds to its charm, making Kottayam a blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.This place is famous for its backwaters and rubber plantations.

Places to Visit

  • Vembanad Lake
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Illikkal Kallu
  • Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple
